Tweety Bird & Sylvester

About

Tweety

"I Tawt I Taw a Puddy Tat.....I did, I Did, I Did Taw a Puddy Tat!"

Tweety appeared in 42 cartoons in the Looney Tunes Golden Age. Despite Tweety's effeminate appearance, Tweety always was male.

Debut

"A Tale of Two Kitties"
1942

Sylvester

"Sufferin' succotash."

Often chasing Tweety, Speedy Gonzales, or Hippety Hopper. When depicted with an owner, he is mainly with Granny; but very early on he would be with Porky Pig.

Debut

"Naughty but Mice"

April 17, 1937
